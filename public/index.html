Organizations

With the API key set, run curl -H "Authorization: Bearer $KEY" https://console.neon.tech/api/v2/users/me/organizations to list your org IDs.

Rates

Neon doesn't expose pricing via API. Each org's rates are auto-detected from its plan and managed_by fields (e.g. Vercel-managed Launch ≠ Neon Launch). For exact precision, click Calibrate next to an org or in the Invoices tab — it derives the per-unit rates from a saved real invoice.
